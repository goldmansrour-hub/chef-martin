Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: March 2026

"If after your first month you haven't found value in Martin's guidance, we will refund you. No questions asked. That is our promise." — Chef Martin Goldman

Free Trial

Every new family starts with a 15-day free trial. Your card is held but not charged during this period. You may cancel at any time before day 15 and you will not be charged anything.

Monthly Subscription

After the trial, Food2Well is billed at £12 per month. You may cancel at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep full access until then.

Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your first paid month, contact us within 30 days of your first charge and we will issue a full refund. No forms, no lengthy process, no questions.

After the first month, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. We always aim to be fair and human about it — just reach out and we will find a solution together.

How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time by:

— Sending a WhatsApp message to Martin saying "I'd like to cancel"
— Emailing us directly at the address below
— Managing your subscription through the Stripe customer portal

What Happens When You Cancel

When you cancel, Martin will stop sending weekly plans and messages at the end of your billing period. Your family's data is retained for 30 days in case you return, then deleted on request.

Exceptions

We do not offer refunds for partial months, or for months where the service was actively used. If there was a technical failure on our side that prevented you from using the service, we will always make it right.

Questions about your subscription or a refund?

We are real people and we respond quickly.
