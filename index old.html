Ah, fermented garlic honey! One of those ancient remedies that tastes like golden medicine and keeps beautifully on the shelf.

• 1 cup raw honey (the cloudier, the better — those wild yeasts are your friends)
• 1 whole head of garlic, cloves separated but unpeeled

The wild yeasts on the garlic skins will wake up and start their slow, magical work. You'll see tiny bubbles dancing around the cloves — that's fermentation saying hello. 🌿
